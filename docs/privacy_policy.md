# Privacy Policy Draft

Trash Tornado is a casual recycling arcade game.

This draft assumes the app does not collect personal information, does not require an account, and does not send gameplay data to a backend. If analytics, ads, crash reporting, purchases, or sign-in are added later, update this policy before release.

## Data Collection

The app stores gameplay progress such as coins, gems, selected skins, upgrades, and unlocked maps on the device.

## Children

Trash Tornado is designed as a family-friendly game. Do not add third-party tracking, behavioral ads, or account features without a child-safety review.
