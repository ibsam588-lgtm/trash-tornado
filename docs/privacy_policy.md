# Privacy Policy Draft

Trash Tornado is a casual recycling arcade game.

This draft assumes the app does not require an account and does not send gameplay progress to a Corsair Labs backend. The hosted Corsair Labs policy should be used for the Play Store listing.

## Data Collection

The app stores gameplay progress such as coins, gems, selected skins, upgrades, and unlocked maps on the device.

## Ads And Third-Party Services

Trash Tornado may show ads through Google Mobile Ads. Depending on consent, region, and SDK behavior, ad services may collect or share approximate location, app interactions, diagnostics, and device or other identifiers for analytics, advertising or marketing, and fraud prevention, security, and compliance.

## Data Deletion

Users can request deletion through https://corsairlabs.com/data-deletion or by emailing labscorsair@gmail.com. Local gameplay progress can also be removed by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.

## Children

Trash Tornado is designed as a family-friendly game. Do not enable behavioral ads, third-party tracking, or account features for child-directed audiences without a child-safety review.
